Soliton Energy was founded to enable entities to engage in solar energy in a simplified, profitable manner.<\/p>\n

We saw that commercial\/industrial companies and real estate owners\/managers wanted the benefits of solar energy, but were hindered by the intimidating complexity of analysis and implementation.<\/p>\n

Moreover, those companies that are committed to reducing their energy costs or investing in solar are being \u201csolared to death\u201d with sales pitches from all sorts of players in the business \u2013 from developers to financiers to product manufacturers.\u00a0 Without a uniform structure and objective evaluation, these proposals are of little use. \u00a0Corporate decision-makers are often stymied by these challenges and information overload.<\/p>\n

Subsequently, fruitful opportunities are often missed, and good projects don\u2019t get built.\u00a0 All the while, utility bills continue to rise while the proposals keep coming.<\/p>\n

At Soliton Energy, we understand the challenges that our clients face as they examine solar energy solutions and deploy them.\u00a0 Our broad, strategic approach gives them the tools and confidence necessary to turn their business goals into reality.<\/p>\n

Every business engagement is different.\u00a0 Every property is different, and every technology has its considerations.\u00a0 Market conditions and government incentives constantly change. \u00a0To keep abreast of all of this can distract you from your core business.<\/p>\n

Let Soliton Energy be your trusted partner if you want to lower your energy costs, or invest in solar energy.<\/p>\n
