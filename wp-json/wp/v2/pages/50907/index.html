Soliton Energy is partnered with a leader in the design and installation of cogeneration<\/a> systems.\u00a0 Natural gas or propane fuels a heat engine to generate electrcity.\u00a0 Exhaust gas heat is recovered through a heat exchanger to heat\/chill water\/air inside a building.\u00a0<\/p>\n

Typical cogeneration models have losses as in any system. The energy distribution below is represented as a percent of total input energy:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Electricity = 45%<\/li>\n
  • Heat + Cooling = 40%<\/li>\n
  • Heat losses = 13%<\/li>\n
  • Electrical line losses = 2%<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Soliton Energy can analyze your present energy situation and help you find the best solution for a cogeneration system.<\/p>\n
