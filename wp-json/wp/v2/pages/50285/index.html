Mark A. Castellano is the Founder of Soliton Energy and brings more than 25 years of sales, marketing, and development experience.<\/p>\n

Most recently, Mr. Castellano was Vice President of Business Development at RenewTricity, a utility-scale solar developer.\u00a0 His clients include large energy consumers, third-party re-sellers, and financial service entities.\u00a0 He managed business development in North America and co-developed and sold a 10 MW solar farm project in New Jersey.<\/p>\n

Mr. Castellano previously held management and technical positions at Texas Instruments, Merrill Lynch, and AT&T Bell Laboratories.\u00a0 He holds a B. in Electrical Engineering from Manhattan College and an M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Princeton University.<\/p>\n
